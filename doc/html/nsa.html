Chapter 6. Novell Support Advisor¶

kueue can create Novell Support Advisor reports for you from supportconfig archives.

Either load an archive from the menu (see screenshot), or create the report right after downloading the archive from the download manager.

Novell Support Advisor itself is not required for that, it works "out of the box".
